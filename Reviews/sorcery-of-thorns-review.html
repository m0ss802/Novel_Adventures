Sorcery of Thorns by Margaret Rogerson Book Review

5 stars

The writing didn't take my breath away like An Enchantment of Ravens, but I loved everything else. I love the character development and the effectiveness of Elisabeth's relationships with Silas and Nathaniel growing. I love the worldbuilding, especially the comfort of the books and library. The plot was interesting and high stakes, but the ending was both full of loss and full of hope. I really wanted a second epilogue for more Nathaniel and Elisabeth though!
